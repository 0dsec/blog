## About the music on this page!

Yeah so I figured I'd just make a quick post about the music that you hear on this page since I just got the visualizer at the top of the page working. All the music you hear on this page was written by me in the last couple of weeks for a game I'm currently working on. It is probably more accurate to say that what you are hearing are demo/early versions of these tracks as I tend to do multiple passes before I'm satisfied. I put these early versions up on the page mostly so I didn't get DMCA'd for my original playlist I had up before the page went live lol.

All these songs are made in Ableton and they're like 90% sampled from classic jungle packs a friend sent to me. For sure used some stuff from the zero g jungle warfare packs and I think some stuff from the Jungle Jungle pack which I think most people who are into sampling will know of. (link to packs at bottom of the post) I did chop up a vocal from some random japanese city pop song I got off youtube for the "level two" track. All drum hits were also either sampled from the source sample or pulled from one of the mentioned packs.

Not everything you hear will make it to the final game. Right now I'm just generating as many loose sketches as I can. Then when I do my next pass I'll cut the track list down to the things that feel and flow right from level to level. From there everything will get reworked and expanded into fully fleshed out tracks considering a lot of these uploads are just simple loops.

Thanks for taking a look! **If you're a game developer who is reading this and you like what you hear feel free to hit me up and lets talk!**
